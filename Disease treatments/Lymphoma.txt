Prevention Note:

There is no sure way to prevent non-Hodgkin lymphoma (NHL). 

Most people with NHL have no risk factors that can be changed, so there is no way to protect against these lymphomas

Treatment:

Active surveillance. Some forms of lymphoma are very slow growing

Chemotherapy uses drugs to destroy fast-growing cells, such as cancer cells

Radiation therapy

Bone marrow transplant
