Prevention:

Avoid and treat hepatitis B and C infections
Limit alcohol and tobacco use
Get to and stay at a healthy weight
Limit exposure to cancer-causing chemicals
Treat diseases that increase liver cancer risk


Treatment:

ablation 
embolization, or both for the liver tumor(s). 

Other options may include:

targeted therapy, 
immunotherapy, 
chemotherapy (either systemic or by hepatic artery infusion),
radiation therapy.
