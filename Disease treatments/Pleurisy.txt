Prevention:

avoiding lung infections and environmental causes of pleurisy such as asbestos exposure.

To prevent infections, wash your hands before touching your face or eating and after using the washroom, and avoid contact with people who have serious respiratory conditions.

Treatment:

treated with antibiotics, and people who are frail or already in poor health may be admitted to hospital. 

Non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), such as ibuprofen, are often used to relieve the chest pain associated with pleurisy


Medication:

Aspirin
Motrin (ibuprofen)	
Tylenol (acetaminophen)
Celebrex (celecoxib)
Prednisone
