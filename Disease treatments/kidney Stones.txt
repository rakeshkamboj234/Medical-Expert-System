Prevention:

Stay hydrated. Drinking more water is the best way to prevent kidney stones
Eat more calcium-rich foods
Eat less sodium
Eat fewer oxalate-rich foods
Eat less animal protein
Avoid vitamin C supplements
Explore herbal remedies


Treatment:

Apple cider vinegar contains acetic acid which helps dissolve kidney stones. 
In addition to flushing out the kidneys, apple cider vinegar can also decrease any pain caused by the stones. 
In addition, water and lemon juice can help flush the stones and prevent future kidney stones
